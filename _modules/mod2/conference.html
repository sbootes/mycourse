Live Session!

Join us each week for a live session on a different topic. You will need a headset with a microphone and speakers to participate in this web conference. Read the critical path or check with your instructor for dates and times.


To join the conference:

  1. Click Collaborate under Course Tools on the side menu then scroll to the name of the conference.
  2. Click the conference name to open the Room Details page.
  3. Click the Join Room button to launch the setup process and follow the prompts.
  4. Click the OK button when the Open Meeting Collaborate page appears.

Note:

You might need to run the Launcher utility when you first join the course. Blackboard will prompt you to "Download the Blackboard Collaborate Launcher".

Miss the session?

One or two days after the live session, you can use the same link to play a recording of the Collaborate session. Remember to select the Recordings tab on the Collaborate page and search for the date of the live broadcast.
