What to expect

About this course

This course runs for 15 weeks over the internet using an online format. It includes a major project, group-work, mid-term and final exam components. Your participation is required at specific times for some of the group activities. Check the Critical Path document for specific dates and times. The final exam must be written in-person; on campus or at one of Humber’s designated testing centres.

During this course, plan for about seven (7) hours of work each week. Class activity (readings, quizzes, discussions and live conferences) should take about three (3) hours per week. Individual activity (homework, tests and team assignments) should take about four (4) hours per week. Individual results will vary. The more organized and familiar you are with the topic, the less time it will take to complete your weekly tasks.

System requirements

For this course you will need:

  • Access to a computer or tablet
  • Access to the internet (preferably high-speeed)
  • A headset for online discussions
  • A web browser supported by Blackboard
  • The Blackboard Collaborate Launcher for web conferencing
  • The Flash plugin from Adobe for some animations
