What to expect

About this course

You have enrolled in an online section of this course. It will be delivered completely online—you do not have to physically come to the College for classes. However, some activities such as group meetings and live conference sessions may require your participation at specific times. Pay close attention to communications from your instructor for these details. The final exam must be written in-person—either on campus or through a virtual proctor at an approved testing location. Your instructor can provide you with details about this as the course progresses.

In order to do well in this course, you can generally expect to complete between 7 and 10 hours of work per week. This will vary throughout the term—with some weeks being lighter or heavier than others. The most important things you can do to manage this workload successfully are to stay organized and to avoid procrastination. Use the Critical Path as your roadmap to stay on track with all course work.

System requirements

For this course you will need:

  • Access to a computer or tablet
  • Access to the internet (preferably high-speeed)
  • A headset for online discussions
  • A web browser supported by Blackboard
  • The Blackboard Collaborate Launcher for web conferencing
  • The Flash plugin from Adobe for some animations
